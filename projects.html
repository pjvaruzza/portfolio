Utility · Grid

Southern California Edison

Distribution Engineer · DER Integration · 2022 – Present

Background

Southern California Edison (SCE) is one of the largest investor-owned utilities in the US, supplying electricity to 15 million people across a 50,000 sq mi service territory. As a Distribution Engineer, my role focuses on accelerating the integration of Distributed Energy Resources (DERs) onto the grid, aligned with the California Public Utility Commission (CPUC) High DER Future Grid Proceeding. I have built a deep understanding of how the utility industry is responding to unprecedented load growth and grid decarbonization challenges.

Key Contributions

  • Distribution grid planning and engineering for DER interconnection at scale
  • Non-wires alternatives cost-benefit analysis to defer traditional infrastructure upgrades
  • Data analysis and process automation to improve planning workflows
  • CPUC regulatory compliance and High DER Future Grid proceeding support
  • Supporting SCE's "Countdown to 2045" climate targets and enabling statewide electrification
  • Cross-functional collaboration with rates, policy, and operations teams

Skills Applied

  • Distribution grid planning & engineering
  • Non-wires alternatives & cost-benefit analysis
  • Project management & data automation
  • CPUC regulatory compliance
Startup · Founder

Hubbub

Founder · Circular Economy Platform · 2020 – 2022

Background

Hubbub.shop is a circular economy startup that makes it cheaper, more convenient, and more enjoyable for consumers to rent everyday items instead of buying new. I launched it during my undergrad at Columbia University, frustrated by the environmental destruction caused by the massive volumes of consumer waste sent to landfill each year. In January 2020, I recruited co-founders united by a vision to scale a circular economic model keeping resources and materials out of landfill.

Key Results

  • Navigated the pre-seed fundraising process, securing capital from a startup incubator to scale NYC operations
  • Served 400+ users per month with year-over-year revenue growth of 288%
  • Kept 210 tonnes of items out of landfill, embodying an estimated 1,320 tonnes of CO₂e avoided
  • Developed a strong unit economics model using micro-fulfillment hubs near target users to minimize delivery costs
  • Built data analytics and logistics tools to forecast demand patterns and automate rental order completion
  • Led rapid product iteration through weekly interviews with dozens of target customers
  • Assembled a cross-functional team covering tech development and operations as we expanded across New York City
